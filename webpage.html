***MUMBAI INDIANS***

Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians (MI) is the Mumbai-based Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise that plays its home matches at Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai. It is the only team to win the IPL title five times (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019 and 2020). Founded in 2008, Mumbai Indians is owned by India's biggest conglomerate, Reliance Industries, through its subsidiary IndiaWin Sports.

Mumbai Indians in IPL 2018

The team had a disastrous start to IPL 2018, as it lost 5 of its 6 matches. However, Rohit Sharma-led side excelled in the later half of the tournament, though that was not enough to book a place in the playoffs.

IPL 2020

Mumbai won its fifth IPL title in 2020 as it defeated Delhi Capitals by 5 wickets in the final. MI ended the league stage with nine wins in 14 matches and emerged as the top team on the points table.
